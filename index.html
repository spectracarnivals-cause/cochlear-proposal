Who We Are

Spectra Carnivals LLP is a Thiruvananthapuram-based event management company dedicated to creating meaningful, community-driven experiences. We believe great events can do more than entertain — they can transform lives.

Beats for a Cause is our flagship initiative, merging the power of live music with social impact by channelling funds directly to cochlear implant children in Kerala.

Our Mission

Kerala has successfully funded cochlear implant surgeries for children. But the journey doesn't end at surgery — processors need replacement, batteries run out, and mapping services are essential for children to maintain their hearing.

Beats for a Cause bridges this gap by raising funds for cochlear device upgrades and long-term maintenance, ensuring children never return to silence.
